What should I check before booking or travelling based on an AI itinerary?

AI-generated itineraries are planning tools — they should be used as a starting point, not a final word. Before booking or travelling, always independently verify:

  • Visa and entry requirements for your nationality
  • Current opening hours and prices for attractions
  • Availability of specific restaurants or hotels before booking
  • Local transport schedules and any recent service changes
  • Current travel advisories for your destination
  • Health and vaccination requirements if applicable

Travel conditions change. An AI itinerary is an expert starting point — verification protects you before you commit.
